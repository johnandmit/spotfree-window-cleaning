Our Story

Built on Hard Work and Honest Service

Spotfree was founded by Luke with a simple mission: deliver exceptional exterior cleaning results and treat every customer like a neighbour.

What started as a one-man window cleaning operation in Auckland has grown into one of the city's highest-rated exterior cleaning services, with over 211 five-star Google reviews and a perfect 5.0 rating.
